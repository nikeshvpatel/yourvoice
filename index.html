APPLICATIONS AND SERVICES

HOSTED PBX

Modern SIP (Session Initiated Protocol) VoIP (Voice over Internet Protocol) solutions which can be tailored to your needs on a project to project basis, for your business or contact centre. Hosted or self-managed turnkey configurations.

VOIP GATEWAY

We offer a fast, affordable SIP termination and origination solution for both conversational and dialer traffic. Our Tier 1 carrier network can be combined with our Nuvana™ PBX or integrated with your existing platform.

SMS

One-Way and Two-Way messaging capability, for SMS and MMS messages. Nuvana SMS will help you communicate easily and quickly with Customers, Consumers, Constituents, in a cost-effective and preferred way.

CHAT

Connect with your Customers on demand. Our live-chat solution is simple, reliable, easy to use! Hosted, secure, and only requires a small snippet of HTML code be added to your website.

DATA SERVICES

Whether you need to find phone numbers, addresses, identify wireless phone numbers, or you want to enhance the data you already have, we have the data to meet your needs.

WEB SERVICES

Safe, secure, reliable web hosting for your website or other business needs. With flexible packages and support available, you can rest assured that we’ll keep you connected and online.
